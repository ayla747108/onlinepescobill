PESCO Project – Bill Calculator & SEO Resources

This repository is dedicated to the PESCO (Peshawar Electric Supply Company) website project, providing a complete set of tools, resources, and strategies to enhance both functionality and online visibility. The main purpose of this repository is to centralize all technical files, articles, SEO materials, and reports that support the growth and optimization of the PESCO web platform onlinepescobill.pk.

At its core, this repository includes a PESCO Bill Calculator tool, designed to help users calculate their electricity bills online with accuracy and ease. Along with the calculator code, detailed instructions and documentation are provided to ensure smooth integration and usage. This feature not only adds value to end-users but also improves engagement on the website.

In addition to technical tools, the repository contains a collection of articles and guides focused on user education. These articles explain how customers can check their PESCO bills, understand billing details, and adopt energy-saving practices. Such content plays a vital role in boosting search engine visibility and improving user experience.

To support long-term growth, the repository features a dedicated section on SEO strategies. This includes both on-page SEO (meta tags, content structure, internal linking) and off-page SEO (backlinks, guest posting, Web 2.0 blogs, social bookmarking). Furthermore, backlink lists and outreach strategies are documented to simplify the process of building authority and trust.

The Reports section offers monthly progress tracking, covering SEO performance, traffic analytics, and optimization results. Finally, the Resources section provides helpful SEO tools, tutorials, and references for continuous learning and improvement.

Overall, this repository is a comprehensive hub for developers, SEO specialists, and digital marketers working on the PESCO project through onlinepescobill.pk. It ensures that all project materials are well-organized, easily accessible, and aligned with the long-term vision of creating a user-friendly and SEO-optimized platform.
